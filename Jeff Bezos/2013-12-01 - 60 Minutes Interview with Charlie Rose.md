# Jeff Bezos - 60 Minutes Interview with Charlie Rose (2013)

**Date:** December 1, 2013  
**Source:** CBS 60 Minutes  
**Interviewer:** Charlie Rose  
**Location:** Amazon Headquarters, Seattle

---

## About This Interview

Bezos revealed Amazon Prime Air (drone delivery) for the first time and discussed the company's relentless focus on innovation and customer experience.

---

## The Interview

### Prime Air Reveal

**Charlie Rose:** You've got something to show me.

**Jeff Bezos:** We have a thing called Amazon Prime Air. The idea is to deliver packages to customers in 30 minutes or less using drones.

*[Shows footage of octocopter delivering package]*

**Rose:** That looks like science fiction.

**Bezos:** It is. But it's also real. This is early days. We need regulatory approval. We need to prove safety. But the technology is there.

**Rose:** When will this happen?

**Bezos:** I think it could happen in four or five years. Maybe sooner if we get the regulatory framework in place.

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Long-Term Vision
Prime Air was presented as future technology, not immediate reality.

### 2. Regulatory Challenges
Acknowledged the hurdles beyond just technology.

### 3. Innovation Pipeline
Demonstrated that Amazon is always working on what comes next.
