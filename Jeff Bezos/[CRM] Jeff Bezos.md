## Content Index

### 1. 1997-04-24 - Amazon IPO Roadshow
**Focus:** Early vision, long-term thinking, investor messaging  
**Key:** "We're not optimizing for short-term profits. We're optimizing for long-term market leadership."

### 2. 1999-10-26 - 60 Minutes Interview
**Focus:** Dot-com bubble skepticism, stock price vs. business  
**Key:** Ignore the stock, focus on the business fundamentals.

### 3. 2003-02-XX - TED Talk: The Electricity of the Internet
**Focus:** Internet adoption curves, long-term technology trends  
**Key:** The most famous Bezos speech comparing Internet to electricity.

### 4. 2010-11-18 - Interview with Charlie Rose
**Focus:** Kindle, innovation culture, Amazon Prime, Blue Origin  
**Key:** "Failure and invention are inseparable twins."

### 5. 2012-12-03 - Interview with Henry Blodget at IGNITION
**Focus:** Low margins, customer obsession, leadership principles  
**Key:** "Willingness to be misunderstood for long periods of time."

### 6. 2013-12-01 - 60 Minutes with Charlie Rose
**Focus:** Prime Air drone reveal  
**Key:** First public demonstration of Amazon's drone delivery vision.

### 7. 2014-06-18 - Fire Phone Interview (Mossberg & Swisher)
**Focus:** Fire Phone launch, failure philosophy  
**Key:** Public example of willingness to fail big.

### 8. 2016-05-24 - Interview with Axel Springer
**Focus:** Day One philosophy, leadership principles, work-life harmony  
**Key:** "Day Two is stasis, followed by irrelevance, followed by excruciating decline, followed by death."

### 9. 2018-09-13 - Interview with David Rubenstein
**Focus:** Regret minimization framework, Washington Post, long-term thinking  
**Key:** Personal story: "I knew I wouldn't regret trying and failing. I would regret never trying."

### 10. 2018-10-03 - Interview with TechCrunch
**Focus:** International expansion, Whole Foods, entering new categories  
**Key:** "Customer obsession is universal. But how you execute changes by market."
