## Core Ideas

### 1. Competition Is For Losers
- Monopolies are good for business, bad for competitors
- Perfect competition drives profits to zero
- Every startup should aim for monopoly
- **See:** CS183 Lecture 5, Startup School

### 2. The Great Stagnation
- Progress in atoms (physical world) stopped in the 1970s
- Only bits (software) advanced
- Cultural/psychological shift from definite to indeterminate optimism
- **See:** Tyler Cowen interview, Jordan Peterson, Ross Douthat

### 3. Zero to One
- Horizontal progress = copying (globalization)
- Vertical progress = creating (technology)
- Startups must do 0→1, not 1→n
- **See:** Zero to One lecture, CS183 notes

### 4. Secrets Exist
- Great businesses are built on secrets
- "What important truth do few agree with?"
- Contrarian thinking is essential
- **See:** All interviews, especially Blake Masters notes
