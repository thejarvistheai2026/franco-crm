## Content Index

### Core Frameworks

**1. 2017 - How to Build an SEO Growth Engine**
SEO as a system: Content → Indexing → Ranking → Clicks → Users
Key: "SEO doesn't work like magic. SEO is a system."

**2. 2018 - Building Growth Loops**
Loops (viral, content, paid) vs funnels
Key: "Funnels don't compound. You have to keep spending to keep growing."

**3. 2019 - Why Product Market Fit Isn't Enough**
PMF is table stakes. You also need product-channel fit, retention, unit economics.
Key: "People treat PMF like a finish line. It's the starting line."

**4. 2019 - The First Week Is Everything**
First week experience determines long-term retention
Key: "Day 2 return rate predicted long-term retention."

**5. 2020-XX-XX - The Adjacent User Theory**
Products grow until they hit user-type ceilings. Remove barriers for adjacent users.
Key: "Products grow until they don't."

**6. 2020-XX-XX - Retention Is All You Need**
The leaky bucket problem: optimize retention before acquisition
Key: "Retention is all you need."

**7. 2021-XX-XX - Product Strategy for Growth**
Product owns all four levers: acquisition, activation, retention, monetization
Key: "Growth isn't separate from product. Growth is what happens when you build the right product."

### Interviews

**8. 2020-XX-XX - Interview with Lenny Rachitsky**
Deep dive into Pinterest growth, Adjacent User Theory, international expansion
Key: "Growth is not just optimization. It's about unlocking adjacent users and removing barriers."

**9. 2022-XX-XX - Interview with Reforge (CPO at Eventbrite)**
Product leadership, growth vs product teams, roadmaps, leadership frameworks
Key: "Growth can't be siloed from product. Full-funnel thinking is required."

## Key Mental Models

1. **Adjacent User** — Growth comes from removing barriers for the next user type
2. **Growth Loops** — Systems where output becomes input
3. **First Week** — Day 2 return predicts long-term retention
4. **Retention Curves** — Curves that flatten indicate PMF
5. **Barrier Removal** — Growth work is removing blocks, not adding features
6. **Four Levers** — Product owns acquisition, activation, retention, monetization
7. **PMF Is Starting Line** — Real work begins after product-market fit
8. **SEO Flywheel** — Content → indexing → ranking → clicks → users → content
