## Key Frameworks

### From "We Replaced Our Sales Team with 20 AI Agents"

1. **The 1.2 Humans Model** - Replace scale roles with 20 agents + 1.2 humans (orchestrated by Chief AI Officer)
2. **Digital Best Salesperson** - Train agents on your #1 performer's scripts, not average reps
3. **30-Day Training Rule** - AI agents require 30+ days of active training before effective
4. **Orchestration Tax** - Someone must spend 1-2 hours daily managing agents
5. **SDR Extinction Timeline** - Email-based SDRs 90% displaced within 12 months
6. **Hyper-Employability** - Become the person who can implement and orchestrate AI agents
7. **The 50% In-Market Phenomenon** - AI tools have 50%+ prospects in-market vs. traditional 3-5%
