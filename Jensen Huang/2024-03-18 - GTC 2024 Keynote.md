## Keynote Transcript Highlights

### Opening: The Computing Revolution

**Jensen Huang:** This is the largest gathering of NVIDIA developers ever. Welcome to GTC.

We're living through a moment in time. The computer is being reinvented. The data center is being reinvented. The entire computing stack is being reinvented.

Generative AI is a new computing platform. Just like PC, internet, mobile, cloud—AI is a new platform that will change every industry.

### Blackwell Architecture Announcement

**Jensen Huang:** We need even bigger GPUs. So ladies and gentlemen, I would like to introduce you to Blackwell.

[Unveils Blackwell]

Blackwell is massive. It has 208 billion transistors. That's more than twice Hopper. But it's not just bigger—it's smarter.

Blackwell has a second-generation transformer engine. FP4 precision support. Second-generation NVLink. And for the first time, a dedicated decompression engine.

But what makes Blackwell revolutionary is the way we increased inference performance. Inference is hard. If inference were easy, everyone would be doing it. It's not easy.

### AI Factories

**Jensen Huang:** We have to build a new type of data center. We call them AI factories.

Traditional data centers process data. AI factories generate intelligence. They produce tokens. They reason. They understand.

An AI factory runs 24/7, generating intelligence for the world. This is a new industrial revolution.

### Computing Platforms: Past and Present

**Jensen Huang:** Let me explain why this is different.

In the past, computing was retrieval-based. You type a query, you get a document. Instruction-based. You write code, the computer executes.

Now computing is generative. You describe what you want, the computer generates it. It's intention-based. You don't code, you prompt.

The computer is now reasoning. The computer is now generating. This is a brand new way of computing.

We've moved from CPU retrieval computing to GPU generative computing. This requires a completely different architecture.

### The Omniverse and Physical AI

**Jensen Huang:** The next wave of AI is physical AI. Robots. Self-driving cars. Manufacturing systems.

For physical AI to work, you need three computers:
1. A computer for training the AI (DGX)
2. A computer for running the AI (the car, the robot)
3. And most importantly, a computer for simulating the AI (Omniverse)

We're building a time machine. It's called simulation. In Omniverse, an AI can experience thousands of years of training in days.

### Humanoid Robotics

**Jensen Huang:** We announced Project GR00T, a general-purpose foundation model for humanoid robots.

The human form factor is incredibly capable. We designed our world around it. Humanoid robots can operate in our world seamlessly.

We're partnering with leading humanoid companies: 1X, Agility Robotics, Boston Dynamics, Figure AI, Fourier Intelligence, Sanctuary AI, Unitree, and XPENG.

### Enterprise AI and NIM

**Jensen Huang:** Every enterprise needs their own AI. We've created NVIDIA NIM—microservices that let companies deploy custom AI models.

NIM lets you connect enterprise data to foundation models securely. It's optimized inference. Every GPU generation gets faster.

This is how we democratize AI. Not everyone can build their own GPUs. But everyone can build their own AI.

### The Sustainability Angle

**Jensen Huang:** I want to make one thing clear: accelerated computing is sustainable computing.

The world is building 100 megawatt, 500 megawatt, gigawatt data centers. If we do this with CPUs alone, the power consumption would be unsustainable.

But with accelerated computing—GPUs—we can reduce power by orders of magnitude. Blackwell delivers 25x better energy efficiency for inference compared to Hopper.

This is the only way forward. Accelerated computing is the sustainable path.

### Closing Statement

**Jensen Huang:** In 1993, we believed accelerated computing would change the world. We believed parallel processing was the future.

Today, 31 years later, that belief is being realized beyond our wildest dreams.

Generative AI is the new computing platform. AI factories are the new data centers. Physical AI is the next frontier.

We're just getting started.

Welcome to GTC.
