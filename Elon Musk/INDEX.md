## Key Themes Across All Transcripts

### 1. First Principles Thinking
Break everything down to physics. Ask "Why has it always been done this way?"

### 2. Manufacturing as Moat
The factory is the product. Gigapress, 4680 cells, vertical integration.

### 3. AI Existential Risk
Consistently warning about AI since 2014. "Far more dangerous than nukes."

### 4. Optimistic Timelines
Consistently aggressive schedules. Eventually achieves the goals.

### 5. Work as Compulsion
Not ambition—obligation. "These things are important."

### 6. Cost Thresholds
$100K Mars, $35K Tesla, $100/kWh battery. Specific price points matter.

### 7. Questioning Orthodoxy
Challenges every industry assumption. Generates enemies, creates disruptions.

## Unique Patterns

### Physics Over Economics
"What do the laws of physics allow?" Economics follows from physics.

### Timelines as Motivation
Aggressive dates create urgency. Miss them, but ship faster than planned approach.

### Public Iteration
Fail publicly, learn, try again. Progress is transparent.

### Compulsion Over Ambition
Not "I want to" but "I have to." The work chooses him.
